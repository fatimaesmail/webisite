✦ My Story

A journey that began with knowing
I was different

Explore My Work

My name is Fatima Esmail and I want to welcome you. My journey as a healer began many years ago when I was a young child, when I came to learn that I was different. I knew I was here on this earth for a greater purpose than simply to have a family and make money.

As I grew, I started taking interest in learning various healing modalities and working with people to help them with their problems. Through this, I realised that healing others also meant healing myself — and looking deeper to my greater purpose, which was to find myself and start my own spiritual journey.

Fate brought me my Guru, who started me on the path of true spirituality. This path is extremely powerful because it helps you see your own power as an individual and how you are connected to the entire universe. All the secrets of the universe begin to reveal themselves from within when you start this journey.

This site exists to help you on your path — to uplift your soul, to heal, to help others, and to transform into the you that you are truly meant to be.

You are a unique individual with unbelievable beauty. Others may not see you. They may not understand you. Do not let anyone steal your joy. Your power, your strength, your courage and your beauty lie within you — not outside of you.
