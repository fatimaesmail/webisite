None at all. The Shift Collective welcomes people at every stage of their journey — whether you're completely new to inner work or have been on this path for years. You are met exactly where you are.

Sanctuary is a beautiful, generous entry point — daily meditations, breathwork, hypnotherapy scripts, Yoga Nidra journeys, sacred practices and community access at $22/month. Full Membership adds the live weekly Cosmic Healing Sessions and Q&As, which is where the deepest transformation happens.

Each session is a live, guided experience combining energy transmission, breathwork, intuitive energy clearing, and sacred ritual. They are powerful, deeply felt, and unlike anything you may have experienced before. Sessions are also recorded so you can watch the replay if you can't attend live.
Can I cancel at any time? +
Absolutely. No contracts, no commitments. You can cancel your membership at any time with no questions asked. We want you here because it's serving you — not because you feel locked in.
Can I upgrade from Sanctuary to Full Membership later? +
Yes, always. Many people start in Sanctuary to get a feel for the community and the energy, and upgrade when they're ready for the full experience. There's no pressure — your journey unfolds at your pace.
